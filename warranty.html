Warranty

Our Quality Guarantee

We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranty coverage.

Workmanship Warranty

Ibex Roofing Solutions provides a 5-year workmanship warranty on all new roof installations and repairs, covering defects in installation.

Manufacturer Warranties

Materials used in our projects are covered by manufacturer warranties, typically ranging from 20 to 50 years, depending on the product.

Warranty Terms

  • Warranties are non-transferable unless specified in your contract.
  • Damage due to natural disasters or improper maintenance is not covered.
  • Claims must be submitted in writing to support@ibexroofing.com.

Contact Us

For warranty claims or inquiries, please contact us at support@ibexroofing.com.
